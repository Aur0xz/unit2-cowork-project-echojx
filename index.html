What are pokèmons?

Pokémon are creatures of all shapes and sizes who live in the wild or alongside their human partners, which for them are trainers. During their adventures, Pokémon grow and become more experienced and even evolve into stronger Pokémon. Discover all of them, and remember: Catch 'em all!
